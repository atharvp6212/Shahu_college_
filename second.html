Hello, welcome to Mahatma Phule Mahavidyalaya, Ahmedpur. I am the Ashok tree, scientifically known as Saraca asoca, and I belong to the Fabaceae family. I am also referred to as the Ashoka tree. I am revered in various cultures for my symbolic significance, often representing love, compassion, and serenity. I have a rich history in traditional medicine, and various parts of my tree, including the bark, leaves, and flowers, have been used for their medicinal properties. I am believed to have therapeutic value in treating and alleviating conditions such as dysentery, gynecological disorders, and skin diseases. Thank you for getting to know me.
