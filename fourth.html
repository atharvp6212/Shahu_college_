Hello and welcome to Mahatma Phule Mahavidyalaya, Ahmedpur. I am the Gulmohar Tree, also known as the Flame Tree or Royal Poinciana. My botanical name is Delonix regia, and I belong to the family Fabaceae. I'm a majestic tree known for my vibrant red or orange flowers. While I may not have the same extensive medicinal properties as the Butterfly Palm, I hold cultural and environmental significance. My stunning blooms bring beauty and shade, and I play a crucial role in landscaping and urban aesthetics. Thank you for acknowledging my presence at your institution.
