Hello, welcome to Mahatma Phule Mahavidyalaya, Ahmedpur. I am the Gulab Tree, known for my exquisite beauty and the sweet fragrance of my blossoms. My botanical name is Rosa, and I belong to the Rosaceae family. I have been cherished for centuries for my ornamental and medicinal properties. My fragrant petals are used to make perfumes, essential oils, and rosewater. Additionally, the petals have been used in traditional medicine for their potential benefits, which include improving skin health, reducing stress, and promoting relaxation. Thank you for having me here.
