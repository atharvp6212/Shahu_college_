Hello, welcome to Mahatma Phule Mahavidyalaya, Ahmedpur. I am the Kanchan Tree, scientifically known as Bauhinia variegata. I belong to the family Fabaceae. I am also known by several other names, including Orchid Tree, Mountain Ebony, Camel's Foot Tree, and Poor Man's Orchid. I hold significant cultural and medicinal value. In traditional medicine, various parts of me are used for treating and managing a range of health issues, such as gastrointestinal disorders, skin problems, and respiratory ailments. Thank you for getting to know me!
