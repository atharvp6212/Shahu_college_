Hello, welcome to Mahatma Phule Mahavidyalaya, Ahmedpur. I am Saptaparni Tree, scientifically known as Alstonia scholaris. I belong to the Apocynaceae family. I am also referred to as Blackboard Tree, Indian Devil Tree, Ditabark, and Milky Pine. Beyond my botanical significance, I have medicinal value in the treatment and alleviation of various diseases and disorders. I am known to be beneficial in managing conditions such as respiratory ailments, fever, digestive disorders, and as an anti-malarial agent. Thank you for getting to know me.
